Public Law 113-158
113th Congress

                                 An Act


 
  To redesignate the railroad station located at 2955 Market Street in 
 Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, commonly known as ``30th Street Station'', 
  as the ``William H. Gray III 30th Street Station''. <<NOTE: Aug. 8, 
                         2014 -  [H.R. 4838]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled, <<NOTE: 49 USC 24909 
note.>> 
SECTION 1. REDESIGNATION.

    The railroad station located at 2955 Market Street in Philadelphia, 
Pennsylvania, commonly known as ``30th Street Station'', shall be known 
and designated as the ``William H. Gray III 30th Street Station''.
SEC. 2. <<NOTE: 49 USC 24909.>>  REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the railroad station referred to in 
section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``William H. Gray III 
30th Street Station''.

    Approved August 8, 2014.
