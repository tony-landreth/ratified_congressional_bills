Public Law 113-85
113th Congress

                            Joint Resolution


 
Providing for the appointment of Risa Lavizzo-Mourey as a citizen regent 
of the Board of Regents of the Smithsonian Institution. <<NOTE: Feb. 21, 
                        2014 -  [S.J. Res. 29]>> 

    Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United 
States of America in Congress assembled, That, in accordance with 
section 5581 of the Revised Statutes of the United States (20 U.S.C. 
43), the vacancy on the Board of Regents of the Smithsonian Institution, 
in the class other than Members of Congress, occurring by reason of the 
expiration of the term of Patricia Q. Stonesifer of Washington, DC, on 
December 21, 2013, is filled by the appointment of Risa Lavizzo-Mourey 
of Pennsylvania. The appointment is for a term of 6 years, beginning on 
the later of December 22, 2013, or the date of enactment of this joint 
resolution.

    Approved February 21, 2014.
