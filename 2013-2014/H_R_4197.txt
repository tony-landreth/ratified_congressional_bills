Public Law 113-170
113th Congress

                                 An Act


 
 To amend title 5, United States Code, to extend the period of certain 
 authority with respect to judicial review of Merit Systems Protection 
       Board decisions relating to whistleblowers, and for other 
           purposes. <<NOTE: Sept. 26, 2014 -  [H.R. 4197]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled, <<NOTE: All Circuit 
Review Extension Act.>> 
SECTION 1. <<NOTE: 5 USC 101 note.>>  SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``All Circuit Review Extension Act''.
SEC. 2. JUDICIAL REVIEW OF MERIT SYSTEMS PROTECTION BOARD 
                    DECISIONS RELATING TO WHISTLEBLOWERS.

    (a) In General.--Section 7703(b)(1)(B) of title 5, United States 
Code, is amended by striking ``2-year'' and inserting ``5-year''.
    (b) Director Review.--Section 7703(d)(2) of such title is amended by 
striking ``2-year'' and inserting ``5-year''.

    Approved September 26, 2014.
