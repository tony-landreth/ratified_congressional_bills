Public Law 110-155
110th Congress

                            Joint Resolution


 
 Providing for the reappointment of Patricia Q. Stonesifer as a citizen 
           regent of the Board of Regents of the Smithsonian 
         Institution. <<NOTE: Dec. 21, 2007 -  [S.J. Res. 8]>> 

    Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United 
States of America in Congress assembled, <<NOTE: Effective date.>> That, 
in accordance with section 5581 of the Revised Statutes (20 U.S.C. 43), 
the vacancy on the Board of Regents of the Smithsonian Institution, in 
the class other than Members of Congress, occurring because of the 
expiration of the term of Patricia Q. Stonesifer of Washington, is 
filled by the reappointment of Patricia Q. Stonesifer, for a term of 6 
years, effective December 22, 2007.

    Approved December 21, 2007.
