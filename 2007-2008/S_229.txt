Public Law 110-45
110th Congress

                                 An Act


 
  To redesignate a Federal building in Albuquerque, New Mexico, as the 
       ``Raymond G. Murphy Department of Veterans Affairs Medical 
              Center''. <<NOTE: July 5, 2007 -  [S. 229]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. REDESIGNATION.

    The Federal building known and designated as the ``Department of 
Veterans Affairs Medical Center'' located at 1501 San Pedro Drive, SE, 
in Albuquerque, New Mexico, shall be known and redesignated as the 
``Raymond G. Murphy Department of Veterans Affairs Medical Center''.
SEC. 2. REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the Federal building referred to in 
section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Raymond G. Murphy 
Department of Veterans Affairs Medical Center''.

    Approved July 5, 2007.
