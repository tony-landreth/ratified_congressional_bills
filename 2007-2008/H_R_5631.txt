Public Law 110-307
110th Congress

                                 An Act


 
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 
  1155 Seminole Trail in Charlottesville, Virginia, as the ``Corporal 
 Bradley T. Arms Post Office Building''. <<NOTE: Aug. 12, 2008 -  [H.R. 
                                5631]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. CORPORAL BRADLEY T. ARMS POST OFFICE BUILDING.

    (a) Designation.--The facility of the United States Postal Service 
located at 1155 Seminole Trail in Charlottesville, Virginia, shall be 
known and designated as the ``Corporal Bradley T. Arms Post Office 
Building''.
    (b) References.--Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, 
paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to 
in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Corporal 
Bradley T. Arms Post Office Building''.

    Approved August 12, 2008.
