Public Law 110-103
110th Congress

                                 An Act


 
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 
  326 South Main Street in Princeton, Illinois, as the ``Owen Lovejoy 
    Princeton Post Office Building''. <<NOTE: Oct. 24, 2007 -  [H.R. 
                                2825]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. OWEN LOVEJOY PRINCETON POST OFFICE BUILDING.

    (a) Designation.--The facility of the United States Postal Service 
located at 326 South Main Street in Princeton, Illinois, shall be known 
and designated as the ``Owen Lovejoy Princeton Post Office Building''.
    (b) References.--Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, 
paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to 
in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Owen 
Lovejoy Princeton Post Office Building''.

    Approved October 24, 2007.
