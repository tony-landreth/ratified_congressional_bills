Public Law 110-341
110th Congress

                            Joint Resolution


 
 To amend Public Law 108-331 to provide for the construction and related 
activities in support of the Very Energetic Radiation Imaging Telescope 
Array System (VERITAS) project in Arizona. <<NOTE: Oct. 3, 2008 -  [S.J. 
                               Res. 35]>> 

    Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United 
States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. LOCATION OF VERITAS PROJECT.

    Public Law 108-331 (118 Stat. 1281) is amended--
            (1) in the long title, by striking ``on Kitt Peak near 
        Tucson, Arizona'' and inserting ``in Arizona''; and
            (2) in section 1, <<NOTE: 20 USC 50 note.>> by striking ``on 
        Kitt Peak near Tucson, Arizona'' and inserting ``at the Fred 
        Lawrence Whipple Observatory Base Camp on Mount Hopkins, 
        Arizona, or other similar location''.

    Approved October 3, 2008.
