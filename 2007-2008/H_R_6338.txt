Public Law 110-397
110th Congress

                                 An Act


 
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 
 4233 West Hillsboro Boulevard in Coconut Creek, Florida, as the ``Army 
SPC Daniel Agami Post Office Building''. <<NOTE: Oct. 13, 2008 -  [H.R. 
                                6338]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. ARMY SPC DANIEL AGAMI POST OFFICE BUILDING.

    (a) Designation.--The facility of the United States Postal Service 
located at 4233 West Hillsboro Boulevard in Coconut Creek, Florida, 
shall be known and designated as the ``Army SPC Daniel Agami Post Office 
Building''.
    (b) References.--Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, 
paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to 
in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Army SPC 
Daniel Agami Post Office Building''.

    Approved October 13, 2008.
