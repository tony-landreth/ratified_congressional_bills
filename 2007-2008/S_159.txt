Public Law 110-1
110th Congress

                                 An Act


 
To redesignate the White Rocks National Recreation Area in the State of 
  Vermont as the ``Robert T. Stafford White Rocks National Recreation 
              Area''. <<NOTE: Jan. 17, 2007 -  [S. 159]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. <<NOTE: 16 USC 460nn-1 note.>> ROBERT T. STAFFORD WHITE ROCKS 
            NATIONAL RECREATION AREA.

    (a) Redesignation.--The White Rocks National Recreation Area in the 
State of Vermont, as established by section 202 of the Vermont 
Wilderness Act of 1984 (16 U.S.C. 460nn-1), is redesignated as the 
``Robert T. Stafford White Rocks National Recreation Area''.
    (b) <<NOTE: 16 USC 460nn, 460nn-1, 460nn-3.>> References.--Any 
reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of 
the United States to the recreation area referred to in subsection (a) 
shall be deemed to be a reference to the Robert T. Stafford White Rocks 
National Recreation Area.

    Approved January 17, 2007.
