Public Law 110-454
110th Congress

                                 An Act


 
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 
  1501 South Slappey Boulevard in Albany, Georgia, as the ``Dr. Walter 
Carl Gordon, Jr. Post Office Building''. <<NOTE: Dec. 19, 2008 -  [H.R. 
                                6859]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. DR. WALTER CARL GORDON, JR. POST OFFICE BUILDING.

    (a) Designation.--The facility of the United States Postal Service 
located at 1501 South Slappey Boulevard in Albany, Georgia, shall be 
known and designated as the ``Dr. Walter Carl Gordon, Jr. Post Office 
Building''.
    (b) References.--Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, 
paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to 
in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Dr. Walter 
Carl Gordon, Jr. Post Office Building''.

    Approved December 19, 2008.
