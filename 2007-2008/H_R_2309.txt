Public Law 110-70
110th Congress

                                 An Act


 
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 
 3916 Milgen Road in Columbus, Georgia, as the ``Frank G. Lumpkin, Jr. 
     Post Office Building''. <<NOTE: Aug. 9, 2007 -  [H.R. 2309]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. FRANK G. LUMPKIN, JR. POST OFFICE BUILDING.

    (a) Designation.--The facility of the United States Postal Service 
located at 3916 Milgen Road in Columbus, Georgia, shall be known and 
designated as the ``Frank G. Lumpkin, Jr. Post Office Building''.
    (b) References.--Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, 
paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to 
in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Frank G. 
Lumpkin, Jr. Post Office Building''.

    Approved August 9, 2007.
