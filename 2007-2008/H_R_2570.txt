Public Law 110-72
110th Congress

                                 An Act


 
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 
  301 Boardwalk Drive in Fort Collins, Colorado, as the ``Dr. Karl E. 
  Carson Post Office Building''. <<NOTE: Aug. 9, 2007 -  [H.R. 2570]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. DR. KARL E. CARSON POST OFFICE BUILDING.

    (a) Designation.--The facility of the United States Postal Service 
located at 301 Boardwalk Drive in Fort Collins, Colorado, shall be known 
and designated as the ``Dr. Karl E. Carson Post Office Building''.
    (b) References.--Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, 
paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to 
in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Dr. Karl E. 
Carson Post Office Building''.

    Approved August 9, 2007.
