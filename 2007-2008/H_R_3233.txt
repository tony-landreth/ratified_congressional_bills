Public Law 110-107
110th Congress

                                 An Act


 
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 
 Highway 49 South in Piney Woods, Mississippi, as the ``Laurence C. and 
 Grace M. Jones Post Office Building''. <<NOTE: Oct. 26, 2007 -  [H.R. 
                                3233]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. LAURENCE C. AND GRACE M. JONES POST OFFICE BUILDING.

    (a) Designation.--The facility of the United States Postal Service 
located at Highway 49 South in Piney Woods, Mississippi, shall be known 
and designated as the ``Laurence C. and Grace M. Jones Post Office 
Building''.
    (b) References.--Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, 
paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to 
in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Laurence C. 
and Grace M. Jones Post Office Building''.

    Approved October 26, 2007.
