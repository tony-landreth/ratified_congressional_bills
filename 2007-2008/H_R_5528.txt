Public Law 110-272
110th Congress

                                 An Act


 
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 
    120 Commercial Street in Brockton, Massachusetts, as the ``Rocky 
Marciano Post Office Building''. <<NOTE: July 15, 2008 -  [H.R. 5528]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. ROCKY MARCIANO POST OFFICE BUILDING.

    (a) Designation.--The facility of the United States Postal Service 
located at 120 Commercial Street in Brockton, Massachusetts, shall be 
known and designated as the ``Rocky Marciano Post Office Building''.
    (b) References.--Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, 
paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to 
in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Rocky 
Marciano Post Office Building''.

    Approved July 15, 2008.
