Public Law 110-119
110th Congress

                            Joint Resolution


 
Providing for the reappointment of Roger W. Sant as a citizen regent of 
 the Board of Regents of the Smithsonian Institution. <<NOTE: Nov. 16, 
                        2007 -  [S.J. Res. 7]>> 

    Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United 
States of America in Congress assembled, That, in accordance with 
section 5581 of the Revised Statutes (20 U.S.C. 43), the vacancy on the 
Board of Regents of the Smithsonian Institution, in the class other than 
Members of Congress, occurring because of the expiration of the term of 
Roger W. Sant of Washington, D.C., is filled by the reappointment of 
Roger W. Sant, for a term of 6 years, effective October 25, 2007.

    Approved November 16, 2007.
