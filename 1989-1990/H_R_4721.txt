H.R.4721
One Hundred First Congress of the United States of America
AT THE SECOND SESSION
Begun and held at the City of Washington on Tuesday, the twenty-third day
of January,
one thousand nine hundred and ninety
An Act
To designate the Federal building located at 340 North Pleasant Valley Road in
Winchester, Virginia, as the `J. Kenneth Robinson Postal Building'.
 Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United
 States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. REDESIGNATION.
 The Federal building located at 340 North Pleasant Valley Road in Winchester,
 Virginia, and known as the Winchester Post Office, shall be known and
 designated as the `J. Kenneth Robinson Postal Building'.
SEC. 2. REFERENCES.
 Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record
 of the United States to the Federal building referred to in section 1 shall
 be deemed to be a reference to the `J. Kenneth Robinson Postal Building'.
Speaker of the House of Representatives.
Vice President of the United States and
President of the Senate.
