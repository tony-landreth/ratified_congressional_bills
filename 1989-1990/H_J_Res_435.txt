H.J.Res.435
One Hundred First Congress of the United States of America
AT THE FIRST SESSION
Begun and held at the City of Washington on Tuesday, the third day of January,
one thousand nine hundred and eighty-nine
Joint Resolution
Making further continuing appropriations for the fiscal year 1990, and for
other purposes.
 Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of
 America in Congress assembled, That section 102(c) of Public Law 101-100,
 as amended by Public Law 101-130, is further amended by striking out
 `November 15, 1989' and inserting in lieu thereof `November 20, 1989'.
Speaker of the House of Representatives.
Vice President of the United States and
President of the Senate.
