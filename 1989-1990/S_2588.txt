S.2588--
One Hundred First Congress of the United States of America
AT THE SECOND SESSION
Begun and held at the City of Washington on Tuesday, the twenty-third day
of January,
one thousand nine hundred and ninety
An Act
To amend section 5948 of title 5, United States Code, to reauthorize physicians
comparability allowances.
 Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United
 States of America in Congress assembled, That section 5948(d) of title 5,
 United States Code, is amended by striking out the second sentence and
 inserting in lieu thereof `No agreement shall be entered into under this
 section later than September 30, 1993, nor shall any agreement cover a
 period of service extending beyond September 30, 1995.'.
Speaker of the House of Representatives.
Vice President of the United States and
President of the Senate.
