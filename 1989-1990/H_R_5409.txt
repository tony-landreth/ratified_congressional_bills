H.R.5409
One Hundred First Congress of the United States of America
AT THE SECOND SESSION
Begun and held at the City of Washington on Tuesday, the twenty-third day
of January,
one thousand nine hundred and ninety
An Act
To designate the Post Office building at 222 West Center Street, in Orem,
Utah, as
the `Arthur V. Watkins Post Office Building'.
 Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United
 States of America in Congress assembled, That the Post Office located at 222
 West Center Street, in Orem, Utah, shall hereafter be known and designated
 as the `Arthur V. Watkins Post Office Building'. Any reference in a law,
 regulation, map, document, record, or other paper of the United States to
 that building shall be deemed to be a reference to the Arthur V. Watkins
 Post Office Building.
Speaker of the House of Representatives.
Vice President of the United States and
President of the Senate.
