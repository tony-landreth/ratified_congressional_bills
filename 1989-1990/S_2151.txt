S.2151
One Hundred First Congress of the United States of America
AT THE SECOND SESSION
Begun and held at the City of Washington on Tuesday, the twenty-third day
of January,
one thousand nine hundred and ninety
An Act
To permit the transfer of the obsolete submarine U.S.S. Requin to the Carnegie
Institute in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, before the expiration of the 60-day
waiting period that would otherwise be applicable to the transfer.
 Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United
 States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. WAIVER OF WAITING PERIOD
 Clauses (2) and (3) of section 7308(c) of title 10, United States Code,
 shall not apply with respect to the transfer by the Secretary of the Navy
 under section 7308(a) of such title of the obsolete submarine U.S.S. Requin
 to the Carnegie Institute in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, for use by the
 Carnegie Institute in connection with the Carnegie Institute Science Center
 in Pittsburgh.
Speaker of the House of Representatives.
Vice President of the United States and
President of the Senate.
