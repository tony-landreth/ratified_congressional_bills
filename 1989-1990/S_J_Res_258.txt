S.J.Res.258
One Hundred First Congress of the United States of America
AT THE SECOND SESSION
Begun and held at the City of Washington on Tuesday, the twenty-third day
of January,
one thousand nine hundred and ninety
Joint Resolution
To authorize the President to proclaim the last Friday of April 1990 as
`National Arbor Day'.
 Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of
 America in Congress assembled, That the President is hereby authorized and
 requested to issue a proclamation designating the last Friday of April 1990
 as `National Arbor Day' and calling upon the people of the United States
 to observe such a day with appropriate ceremonies and activities.
Speaker of the House of Representatives.
Vice President of the United States and
President of the Senate.
