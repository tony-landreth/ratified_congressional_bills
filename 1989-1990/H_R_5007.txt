H.R.5007
One Hundred First Congress of the United States of America
AT THE SECOND SESSION
Begun and held at the City of Washington on Tuesday, the twenty-third day
of January,
one thousand nine hundred and ninety
An Act
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at
100 South John F. Kennedy Drive, Carpentersville, Illinois, as the `Robert
McClory Post Office Building'.
 Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United
 States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. DESIGNATION.
 The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 100 South John
 F. Kennedy Drive, Carpentersville, Illinois, is designated as the `Robert
 McClory Post Office Building'.
SEC. 2. LEGAL REFERENCES.
 Any reference in any law, regulation, document, record, map, or other paper
 of the United States to the facility referred to in section 1 is deemed to
 be a reference to the `Robert McClory Post Office Building'.
Speaker of the House of Representatives.
Vice President of the United States and
President of the Senate.
