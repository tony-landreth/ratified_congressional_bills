One Hundred First Congress of the United States of America
AT THE SECOND SESSION
Begun and held at the City of Washington on Tuesday, the twenty-third day
of January,
one thousand nine hundred and ninety
An Act
To amend title 5 of the United States Code to increase the allowance for
services of
attendants.
  Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United
  States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.
 This Act may be cited as the `Attendant Allowance Adjustment Act'.
SEC. 2. INCREASE IN AMOUNT.
 Subsection (a) of section 8111 of title 5, United States Code is amended
 by striking out `$500' and inserting in lieu thereof `$1,500'.
SEC. 3. EFFECTIVE DATE.
 The amendment made by section 2 shall take effect October 1, 1990.
