S.2806
One Hundred First Congress of the United States of America
AT THE SECOND SESSION
Begun and held at the City of Washington on Tuesday, the twenty-third day
of January,
one thousand nine hundred and ninety
An Act
To redesignate The National System of Interstate and Defense Highways as The
Dwight D. Eisenhower System of Interstate and Defense Highways.
 Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United
 States of America in Congress assembled, That--
 (a) notwithstanding any other provision of law, The National System of
 Interstate and Defense Highways shall be redesignated as `The Dwight
 D. Eisenhower System of Interstate and Defense Highways'; and
 (b) any reference before the date of enactment of this Act in any provision of
 law, regulation, map, sign, or otherwise to The National System of Interstate
 and Defense Highways shall be deemed to refer, on and after such date,
 to The Dwight D. Eisenhower System of Interstate and Defense Highways.
Speaker of the House of Representatives.
Vice President of the United States and
President of the Senate.
