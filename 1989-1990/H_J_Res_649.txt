One Hundred First Congress of the United States of America
AT THE SECOND SESSION
Begun and held at the City of Washington on Tuesday, the twenty-third day
of January,
one thousand nine hundred and ninety
Joint Resolution
Approving the extension of nondiscriminatory treatment (most favored nation
treat-
ment) to the products of Czechoslovakia.
 Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of
 America in Congress assembled, That the Congress approves the extension of
 nondiscriminatory treatment with respect to the products of Czechoslovakia
