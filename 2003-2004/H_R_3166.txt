Public Law 108-165
108th Congress

                                 An Act


 
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 
57 Old Tappan Road in Tappan, New York, as the ``John G. Dow Post Office 
           Building''. <<NOTE: Dec. 6, 2003 -  [H.R. 3166]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. DESIGNATION.

    The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 57 Old 
Tappan Road in Tappan, New York, shall be known and designated as the 
``John G. Dow Post Office Building''.

SEC. 2. REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the facility referred to in section 1 
shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``John G. Dow Post Office 
Building''.

    Approved December 6, 2003.
