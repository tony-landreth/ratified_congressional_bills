Public Law 108-104
108th Congress

                            Joint Resolution


 
 Making further continuing appropriations for the fiscal year 2004, and 
     for other purposes. <<NOTE: Oct. 31, 2003 -  [H.J. Res. 75]>> 

    Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United 
States of America in Congress assembled, That Public Law 108-84 is 
amended by striking the date specified in section <<NOTE: Ante., p. 
1043.>> 107(c) and inserting ``November 7, 2003''.

    Sec. 2. Public Law 108-84 is further amended as follows:
            (1) In section 103, <<NOTE: Ante., p. 1043.>> by inserting 
        ``(a)'' after the section designation and by adding at the end 
        the following new subsection:

    ``(b) For purposes of section 101, the term `rate for operations not 
exceeding the current rate' has the meaning given such term (including 
supplemental appropriations and rescissions) in the attachments to 
Office of Management and Budget Bulletin No. 03-05 entitled 
`Apportionment of the Continuing Resolution(s) for Fiscal Year 2004'.''.
            (2) In section 125, <<NOTE: Ante., p. 1045.>> by inserting 
        before the period at the end the following:

``: Provided, That such amounts as may be necessary for administrative 
expenses of the Grants-in-aid for Airports program shall be available to 
the Secretary of Transportation out of the Airport and Airway Trust Fund 
at a rate for operations not exceeding the current rate and for which 
authority was made available under the Department of Transportation and 
Related Agencies Appropriations Act, 2003''.
            (3) By striking sections 126 through 130 <<NOTE: Ante., p. 
        1045, 1046.3Ante., p. 1046-1048.>> and by redesignating sections 
        131 through 135 as sections 126 through 130, respectively.
            (4) In <<NOTE: Ante., p. 1047.>> section 127, as so 
        redesignated, by striking ``through 130, and section 134,'' and 
        inserting ``and 129''.

    Sec. 3. Section 8144(b) of the Department of Defense Appropriations 
Act, 2003 (Public Law 107-248), as amended by Public Law 108-
84, <<NOTE: Ante., p. 1048.>> is further amended by striking ``October 
31, 2003'' and inserting ``November 7, 2003''.

    Approved October 31, 2003.
