Public Law 108-407
108th Congress

                                 An Act


 
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 
  11110 Sunset Hills Road in Reston, Virginia, as the ``Martha Pennino 
     Post Office Building''. <<NOTE: Oct. 30, 2004 -  [H.R. 5133]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. DESIGNATION.

    The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 11110 
Sunset Hills Road in Reston, Virginia, shall be known and designated as 
the ``Martha Pennino Post Office Building''.

SEC. 2. REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the facility referred to in section 1 
shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Martha Pennino Post Office 
Building''.

    Approved October 30, 2004.
