Public Law 108-348
108th Congress

                                 An Act


 
To authorize the Gateway Arch in St. Louis, Missouri, to be illuminated 
 by pink lights in honor of breast cancer awareness month. <<NOTE: Oct. 
                        20, 2004 -  [S. 2895]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. ILLUMINATION OF GATEWAY ARCH IN HONOR OF BREAST CANCER 
                              AWARENESS MONTH.

    In honor of breast cancer awareness month, the Secretary of the 
Interior shall authorize the Gateway Arch in St. Louis, Missouri, to be 
illuminated by pink lights for a certain period of time in October, to 
be designated by the Secretary of the Interior.

    Approved October 20, 2004.
