Public Law 108-467
108th Congress

                                 An Act


 
 To designate the Federal building and United States courthouse located 
 at 615 East Houston Street in San Antonio, Texas, as the ``Hipolito F. 
Garcia Federal Building and United States Courthouse''. <<NOTE: Dec. 21, 
                         2004 -  [H.R. 3884]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. DESIGNATION.

    The Federal building and United States courthouse located at 615 
East Houston Street in San Antonio, Texas, shall be known and designated 
as the ``Hipolito F. Garcia Federal Building and United States 
Courthouse''.

SEC. 2. REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the Federal building and United States 
courthouse referred to in section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference to 
the ``Hipolito F. Garcia Federal Building and United States 
Courthouse''.

    Approved December 21, 2004.
