Public Law 108-57
108th Congress

                                 An Act


 
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 
 120 Baldwin Avenue in Paia, Maui, Hawaii, as the ``Patsy Takemoto Mink 
     Post Office Building''. <<NOTE: July 14, 2003 -  [H.R. 2030]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. DESIGNATION.

    The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 120 
Baldwin Avenue in Paia, Maui, Hawaii, shall be known and designated as 
the ``Patsy Takemoto Mink Post Office Building''.

SEC. 2. REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the facility referred to in section 1 
shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Patsy Takemoto Mink Post 
Office Building''.

    Approved July 14, 2003.
