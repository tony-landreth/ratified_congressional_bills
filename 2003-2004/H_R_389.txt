Public Law 108-41
108th Congress

                                 An Act


 
To authorize the use of certain grant funds to establish an information 
  clearinghouse that provides information to increase public access to 
    defibrillation in schools. <<NOTE: July 1, 2003 -  [H.R. 389]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress <<NOTE: Automatic Defibrillation in 
Adam's Memory Act. 42 USC 201 note.>> assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Automatic Defibrillation in Adam's 
Memory Act''.

SEC. 2. AMENDMENT TO PUBLIC HEALTH SERVICE ACT.

    Subsection (c) of section 312 of the Public Health Service Act (42 
U.S.C. 244), as amended by Public Law 107-188, is amended--
            (1) at the end of paragraph (5), by striking ``and'';
            (2) by redesignating paragraph (6) as paragraph (7); and
            (3) by inserting after paragraph (5) the following:
            ``(6) establish an information clearinghouse that provides 
        information to increase public access to defibrillation in 
        schools; and''.

    Approved July 1, 2003.
