Public Law 108-399
108th Congress

                                 An Act


 
  To amend the Federal Water Pollution Control Act to reauthorize the 
    National Estuary Program. <<NOTE: Oct. 30, 2004 -  [H.R. 4731]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. REAUTHORIZATION OF NATIONAL ESTUARY PROGRAM.

    Section 320(i) of the Federal Water Pollution Control Act (33 U.S.C. 
1330(i)) is amended by striking ``2005'' and inserting ``2010''.

    Approved October 30, 2004.
