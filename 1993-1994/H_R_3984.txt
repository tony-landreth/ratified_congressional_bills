                                 An Act

  
 
  To designate the building located at 216 Coleman Avenue in Waveland, 
Mississippi, for the period of time during which it houses operations of 
the United States Postal Service, as the ``John Longo, Jr. Post 
Office''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. DESIGNATION.

    The building located at 216 Coleman Avenue in Waveland, 
Mississippi, shall, for the period of time during which it houses 
operations of the United States Postal Service, be known and designated 
as the ``John Longo, Jr. Post Office''.

SEC. 2. REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the building referred to in section 1 
shall, with respect to the period referred to in section 1, be deemed 
to be a reference to the John Longo, Jr. Post Office.
