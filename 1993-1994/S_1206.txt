                                 An Act

  
 
  To redesignate the Federal building located at 380 Trapelo Road in 
Waltham,


      Massachusetts, as the ``Frederick C. Murphy Federal Center''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. REDESIGNATION.

    The Federal building located at 380 Trapelo Road in Waltham, 
Massachusetts, and known as the Waltham Federal Center, shall be known 
and designated as the ``Frederick C. Murphy Federal Center''.

SEC. 2. REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the Federal building referred to in 
section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Frederick C. 
Murphy Federal Center''.
