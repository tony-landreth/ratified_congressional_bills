                                 An Act

  
 
  To designate the Federal building in Jacksonville, Florida, as the 
``Charles E.

                       Bennett Federal Building''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. DESIGNATION.

    The Federal building at 400 Bay Street in Jacksonville, Florida, is 
designated as the ``Charles E. Bennett Federal Building''. If a new 
Federal building is built in Jacksonville, Florida, to replace the 
building at 400 Bay Street, the new Federal building shall be designated 
as the ``Charles E. Bennett Federal Building''.

SEC. 2. LEGAL REFERENCES.

    Any reference in any law, regulation, document, record, map, or 
other paper of the United States to the Federal building referred to in 
section 1 is deemed to be a reference to the ``Charles E. Bennett 
Federal Building''.
