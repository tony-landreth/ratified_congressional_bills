                                 An Act

  
 
  To designate the Post Office building located at 401 E. South Street 
in Jackson,


         Mississippi, as the ``Medgar Wiley Evers Post Office''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. DESIGNATION.

    The Post Office building located at 401 E. South Street in Jackson, 
Mississippi, shall be known and designated as the ``Medgar Wiley Evers 
Post Office''.

SEC. 2. REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the building referred to in section 1 
shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Medgar Wiley Evers Post 
Office''.
