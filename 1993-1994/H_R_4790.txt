                                 An Act

  
 
  To designate the United States courthouse under construction in St. 
Louis, Missouri, as the ``Thomas F. Eagleton United States Courthouse''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. DESIGNATION.

    The United States courthouse under construction at 111 South Tenth 
Street in St. Louis, Missouri, shall be known and designated as the 
``Thomas F. Eagleton United States Courthouse''.

SEC. 2. REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the United States courthouse referred to 
in section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Thomas F. 
Eagleton United States Courthouse''.
