                                 An Act

  
 
  To amend the Omnibus Budget Reconciliation Act of 1993 to permit the 
prompt sharing of timber sale receipts of the Forest Service and the 
Bureau of Land Management.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. TECHNICAL CORRECTION REGARDING SHARING OF TIMBER SALE 
              RECEIPTS.

    (a) Forest Service.--Section 13982(b)(1) of the Omnibus Budget 
Reconciliation Act of 1993 (Public Law 103-66; 107 Stat. 681; 16 U.S.C. 
500 note) is amended by inserting ``out of any money in the Treasury 
not otherwise appropriated,'' before ``for the benefit of counties''.
    (b) Bureau of Land Management.--Section 13983(b)(1) of the Omnibus 
Budget Reconciliation Act of 1993 (Public Law 103-66; 107 Stat. 682; 43 
U.S.C. 1181f note) is amended by inserting ``, out of any money in the 
Treasury not otherwise appropriated,'' after ``shall make payments''.
