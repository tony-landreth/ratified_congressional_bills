                                 An Act

  
 
  To designate the United States Post Office building located at 103-104 
Estate Richmond in Saint Croix, Virgin Islands, as the ``Wilbert 
Armstrong Post Office''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. DESIGNATION.

    The United States Post Office building located at 103-104 Estate 
Richmond in Saint Croix, Virgin Islands, shall be known and designated 
as the ``Wilbert Armstrong Post Office''.

SEC. 2. REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the building referred to in section 1 
shall be deemed to be a reference to the Wilbert Armstrong Post Office.
