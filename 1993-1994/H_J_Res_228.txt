                       One Hundred Third Congress

                                 of the

                        United States of America


                          AT THE FIRST SESSION

          Begun and held at the City of Washington on Tuesday,
  the fifth day of January, one thousand nine hundred and ninety-three


                            Joint Resolution

  
 
  To approve the extension of nondiscriminatory treatment with respect 
to the products

                               of Romania.

    Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United 
States of America in Congress assembled, That the Congress approves the 
extension of nondiscriminatory treatment with respect to the products of 
Romania transmitted by the President to the Congress on July 2, 1993.
