                                 An Act

  
 
  To designate the Federal Building and United States Courthouse located 
at 402 East State Street in Trenton, New Jersey, as the ``Clarkson S. 
Fisher Federal Building and United States Courthouse''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. DESIGNATION.

    The Federal Building and United States Courthouse located at 402 
East State Street in Trenton, New Jersey, shall be known and designated 
as the ``Clarkson S. Fisher Federal Building and United States 
Courthouse''.

SEC. 2. REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the Federal Building and United States 
Courthouse referred to in section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference to 
the ``Clarkson S. Fisher Federal Building and United States 
Courthouse''.
