                                 An Act

  
 
  To provide that the National Education Commission on Time and Learning 
shall


                    terminate on September 30, 1994.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. TERMINATION OF THE NATIONAL EDUCATION COMMISSION ON TIME AND 
LEARNING.
    Subsection (g) of section 102 of the National Education Commission 
on Time and Learning Act (20 U.S.C. 1221-1 note) is amended by striking 
``90 days after submitting the final report required by subsection 
(d)'' and inserting ``on September 30, 1994''.
