                                 An Act


 
          To modify the boundary of Hot Springs National Park.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled, That the boundary of Hot 
Springs National Park is modified as depicted on the map entitled 
``Proposed Boundary Map'', numbered 128/80015, and dated August 5, 1985.
