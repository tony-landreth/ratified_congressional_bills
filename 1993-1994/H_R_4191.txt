                                 An Act

  
 
  To designate the United States Post Office building located at 9630 
Estate Thomas in Saint Thomas, Virgin Islands, as the ``Aubrey C. Ottley 
Post Office''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. DESIGNATION.

    The United States Post Office building located at 9630 Estate 
Thomas in Saint Thomas, Virgin Islands, shall be known and designated 
as the ``Aubrey C. Ottley Post Office''.

SEC. 2. REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the building referred to in section 1 
shall be deemed to be a reference to the Aubrey C. Ottley Post Office.
