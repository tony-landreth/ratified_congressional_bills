                                 An Act


 
To authorize appropriations for the American Folklife Center for fiscal 
                          years 1994 and 1995.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. AUTHORIZATION OF APPROPRIATIONS FOR THE AMERICAN FOLKLIFE 
              CENTER.

    Section 8 of the American Folklife Preservation Act (20 U.S.C. 2107) 
is amended--
        (1) by striking out ``and'' after ``September 30, 1992,''; and
        (2) by inserting after ``September 30, 1993'' the following: ``, 
    $1,120,000 for the fiscal year ending September 30, 1994, and 
    $1,120,000 for the fiscal year ending September 30, 1995''.
