                                 An Act

  
 
  To redesignate the Post Office building located at 1000 Lamar Street 
in Wichita Falls, Texas, as the ``Graham B. Purcell, Jr. Post Office 
Building''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. REDESIGNATION.

    The Post Office building located at 1000 Lamar Street in Wichita 
Falls, Texas, and known as the Main Post Office, shall be known and 
designated as the ``Graham B. Purcell, Jr. Post Office Building''.

SEC. 2. REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the Post Office building referred to in 
section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference to the Graham B. Purcell, 
Jr. Post Office Building.
