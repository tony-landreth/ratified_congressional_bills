                                 An Act

  
 
  To designate the United States Post Office building located at 3000 
Veterans Drive in Saint Thomas, Virgin Islands, as the ``Arturo R. 
Watlington, Sr. Post Office''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. DESIGNATION.

    The United States Post Office building located at 3000 Veterans 
Drive in Saint Thomas, Virgin Islands, shall be known and designated as 
the ``Arturo R. Watlington, Sr. Post Office''.

SEC. 2. REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the building referred to in section 1 
shall be deemed to be a reference to the Arturo R. Watlington, Sr. Post 
Office.
