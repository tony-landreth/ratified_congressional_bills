                                 An Act


 
            Making a technical amendment of the Clayton Act.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. TECHNICAL AMENDMENT OF THE CLAYTON ACT.

    Section 8(a)(5) of the Clayton Act (15 U.S.C. 19(a)(5)) is amended 
by striking ``October 30'' and inserting ``January 31''.
