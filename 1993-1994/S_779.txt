                                 An Act

  
 
  To continue the authorization of appropriations for the East Court of 
the National

           Museum of Natural History, and for other purposes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. NATIONAL MUSEUM OF NATURAL HISTORY.

    (a) In General.--Section 2 of the Act entitled ``An Act to authorize 
the Board of Regents of the Smithsonian Institution to plan, design, 
construct, and equip space in the East Court of the National Museum of 
Natural History building, and for other purposes'', approved October 24, 
1990 (20 U.S.C. 50 note), is amended by inserting ``and succeeding 
fiscal years'' after ``1991''.
    (b) Effective Date.--The amendment made by subsection (a) shall take 
effect as of October 24, 1990.
