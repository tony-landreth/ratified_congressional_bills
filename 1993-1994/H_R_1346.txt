                                 An Act

  
 
  To designate the Federal building located on St. Croix, Virgin 
Islands, as the


               ``Almeric L. Christian Federal Building''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. DESIGNATION.

    The Federal building located on St. Croix, Virgin Islands, shall be 
known and designated as the ``Almeric L. Christian Federal Building''.

SEC. 2. REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the Federal building referred to in 
section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Almeric L. 
Christian Federal Building''.
