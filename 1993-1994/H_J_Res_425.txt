                            Joint Resolution

  
 
  Providing for the convening of the First Session of the One Hundred 
Fourth Congress.

    Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United 
States of America in Congress assembled, That the first regular session 
of the One Hundred Fourth Congress shall begin at noon on Wednesday, 
January 4, 1995.
