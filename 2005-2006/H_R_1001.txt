Public Law 109-36
109th Congress

                                 An Act


 
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 
    301 South Heatherwilde Boulevard in Pflugerville, Texas, as the 
  ``Sergeant Byron W. Norwood Post Office Building''. <<NOTE: July 21, 
                         2005 -  [H.R. 1001]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. DESIGNATION.

    The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 301 
South Heatherwilde Boulevard in Pflugerville, Texas, shall be known and 
designated as the ``Sergeant Byron W. Norwood Post Office Building''.

SEC. 2. REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the facility referred to in section 1 
shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Sergeant Byron W. Norwood 
Post Office Building''.

    Approved July 21, 2005.
