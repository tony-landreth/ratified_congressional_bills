Public Law 109-322
109th Congress

                                 An Act


 
        To reauthorize the North American Wetlands Conservation 
              Act. <<NOTE: Oct. 11, 2006 -  [H.R. 5539]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress <<NOTE: North American Wetlands 
Conservation Reauthorization Act of 2006. 16 USC 4401 note.>> assembled,
SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``North American Wetlands Conservation 
Reauthorization Act of 2006''.
SEC. 2. AUTHORIZATION OF APPROPRIATIONS.

    Section 7(c) of the North American Wetlands Conservation Act (16 
U.S.C. 4406(c)) is amended by striking ``fiscal year 2007'' and 
inserting ``each of fiscal years 2008 through 2012''.

    Approved October 11, 2006.
