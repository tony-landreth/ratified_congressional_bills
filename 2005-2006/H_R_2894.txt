Public Law 109-189
109th Congress

                                 An Act


 
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 
  102 South Walters Avenue in Hodgenville, Kentucky, as the ``Abraham 
      Lincoln Birthplace Post Office Building''. <<NOTE: Mar. 20, 
                         2006 -  [H.R. 2894]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. DESIGNATION.

    The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 102 
South Walters Avenue in Hodgenville, Kentucky, shall be known and 
designated as the ``Abraham Lincoln Birthplace Post Office Building''.

SEC. 2. REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the facility referred to in section 1 
shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Abraham Lincoln Birthplace 
Post Office Building''.

    Approved March 20, 2006.
