Public Law 109-335
109th Congress

                                 An Act


 
 To designate the Federal building and United States courthouse located 
   at 2 South Main Street in Akron, Ohio, as the ``John F. Seiberling 
   Federal Building and United States Courthouse''. <<NOTE: Oct. 12, 
                         2006 -  [H.R. 6051]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. DESIGNATION.

    The Federal building and United States courthouse located at 2 South 
Main Street in Akron, Ohio, shall be known and designated as the ``John 
F. Seiberling Federal Building and United States Courthouse''.
SEC. 2. REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the Federal building and United States 
courthouse referred to in section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference to 
the ``John F. Seiberling Federal Building and United States 
Courthouse''.

    Approved October 12, 2006.
