Public Law 109-348
109th Congress

                                 An Act


 
     To designate the Investigations Building of the Food and Drug 
   Administration located at 466 Fernandez Juncos Avenue in San Juan, 
     Puerto Rico, as the ``Andres Toro Building''. <<NOTE: Oct. 13, 
                         2006 -  [H.R. 5026]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. DESIGNATION.

    The Investigations Building of the Food and Drug Administration 
located at 466 Fernandez Juncos Avenue in San Juan, Puerto Rico, shall 
be known and designated as the ``Andres Toro Building''.
SEC. 2. REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the Federal building referred to in 
section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Andres Toro 
Building''.

    Approved October 13, 2006.
