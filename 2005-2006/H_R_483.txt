Public Law 109-16
109th Congress

                                 An Act


 
 To designate a United States courthouse in Brownsville, Texas, as the 
         ``Reynaldo G. Garza and Filemon B. Vela United States 
          Courthouse''. <<NOTE: June 29, 2005 -  [H.R. 483]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. DESIGNATION.

    The United States courthouse located at the corner of Seventh Street 
and East Jackson Street in Brownsville, Texas, shall be designated and 
known as the ``Reynaldo G. Garza and Filemon B. Vela United States 
Courthouse''.

SEC. 2. REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the United States courthouse referred to 
in section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Reynaldo G. 
Garza and Filemon B. Vela United States Courthouse''.

    Approved June 29, 2005.
