Public Law 109-22
109th Congress

                                 An Act


 
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 
  30777 Rancho California Road in Temecula, California, as the ``Dalip 
   Singh Saund Post Office Building''. <<NOTE: July 12, 2005 -  [H.R. 
                                 120]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. DESIGNATION.

    The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 30777 
Rancho California Road in Temecula, California, shall be known and 
designated as the ``Dalip Singh Saund Post Office Building''.

SEC. 2. REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the facility referred to in section 1 
shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Dalip Singh Saund Post Office 
Building''.

    Approved July 12, 2005.
