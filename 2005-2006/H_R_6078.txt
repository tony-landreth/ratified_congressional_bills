Public Law 109-407
109th Congress

                                 An Act


 
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 
 307 West Wheat Street in Woodville, Texas, as the ``Chuck Fortenberry 
     Post Office Building''. <<NOTE: Dec. 18, 2006 -  [H.R. 6078]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. CHUCK FORTENBERRY POST OFFICE BUILDING.

    (a) Designation.--The facility of the United States Postal Service 
located at 307 West Wheat Street in Woodville, Texas, shall be known and 
designated as the ``Chuck Fortenberry Post Office Building''.
    (b) References.--Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, 
paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to 
in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Chuck 
Fortenberry Post Office Building''.

    Approved December 18, 2006.
