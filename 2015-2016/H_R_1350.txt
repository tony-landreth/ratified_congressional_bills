Public Law 114-35
114th Congress

                                 An Act


 
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 
 442 East 167th Street in Bronx, New York, as the ``Herman Badillo Post 
       Office Building''. <<NOTE: July 20, 2015 -  [H.R. 1350]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. HERMAN BADILLO POST OFFICE BUILDING.

    (a) Designation.--The facility of the United States Postal Service 
located at 442 East 167th Street in Bronx, New York, shall be known and 
designated as the ``Herman Badillo Post Office Building''.
    (b) References.--Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, 
paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to 
in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Herman 
Badillo Post Office Building''.

    Approved July 20, 2015.
