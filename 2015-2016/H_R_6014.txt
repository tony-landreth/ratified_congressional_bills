Public Law 114-307
114th Congress

                                 An Act


 
  To allow the Administrator of the Federal Aviation Administration to 
         enter into reimbursable agreements for certain airport 
            projects. <<NOTE: Dec. 16, 2016 -  [H.R. 6014]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. <<NOTE: Notice. 49 USC 47101 note.>> REIMBURSABLE 
                              AGREEMENTS FOR CERTAIN AIRPORT 
                              PROJECTS.

    The Administrator of the Federal Aviation Administration may enter 
into a reimbursable agreement with a State or local government agency to 
carry out a project at an airport as to which notice is required under 
section 77.9 of title 14, Code of Federal Regulations, if the 
agreement--
            (1) includes measures for cost-effective completion of such 
        project; and
            (2) would not negatively affect the safety or efficiency of 
        the national airspace system.

    Approved December 16, 2016.
