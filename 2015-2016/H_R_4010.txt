Public Law 114-204
114th Congress

                                 An Act


 
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 
 522 North Central Avenue in Phoenix, Arizona, as the ``Ed Pastor Post 
            Office''. <<NOTE: July 29, 2016 -  [H.R. 4010]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. ED PASTOR POST OFFICE.

    (a) Designation.--The facility of the United States Postal Service 
located at 522 North Central Avenue in Phoenix, Arizona, shall be known 
and designated as the ``Ed Pastor Post Office''.
    (b) References.--Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, 
paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to 
in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Ed Pastor 
Post Office''.

    Approved July 29, 2016.
