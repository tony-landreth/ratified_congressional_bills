Public Law 114-107
114th Congress

                                 An Act


 
 To exempt for an additional 4-year period, from the application of the 
 means-test presumption of abuse under chapter 7, qualifying members of 
reserve components of the Armed Forces and members of the National Guard 
who, after September 11, 2001, are called to active duty or to perform a 
 homeland defense activity for not less than 90 days. <<NOTE: Dec. 18, 
                         2015 -  [H.R. 4246]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled, <<NOTE: National Guard 
and Reservist Debt Relief Extension Act of 2015. 11 USC 101 note.>> 
SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``National Guard and Reservist Debt 
Relief Extension Act of 2015''.
SEC. 2. NATIONAL GUARD AND RESERVISTS DEBT RELIEF AMENDMENT.

    Section 4(b) of the National Guard and Reservists Debt Relief Act of 
2008 (Public Law 110-438; 122 Stat. 5000) <<NOTE: 11 USC 707 note. 122 
Stat. 5002.>> is amended by striking ``7-year'' and inserting ``11-
year''.

    Approved December 18, 2015.
