Public Law 114-9
114th Congress

                            Joint Resolution


 
  Providing for the reappointment of David M. Rubenstein as a citizen 
           regent of the Board of Regents of the Smithsonian 
         Institution. <<NOTE: Apr. 7, 2015 -  [H.J. Res. 10]>> 

    Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United 
States of America in Congress assembled, That, in accordance with 
section 5581 of the Revised Statutes (20 U.S.C. 43), the vacancy on the 
Board of Regents of the Smithsonian Institution, in the class other than 
Members of Congress, occurring by reason of the expiration of the term 
of David M. Rubenstein of Maryland on May 7, 2015, is filled by the 
reappointment of the incumbent. The <<NOTE: Effective 
date.>> reappointment is for a term of 6 years, beginning on May 8, 
2015, or the date of the enactment of this joint resolution, whichever 
occurs later.

    Approved April 7, 2015.
