S.J.Res. 64
One Hundred Second Congress of the United States of America
AT THE FIRST SESSION
Begun and held at the City of Washington on Thursday, the third day of January,
one thousand nine hundred and ninety-one
Joint Resolution
To authorize the President to proclaim the last Friday of April 1991, as
`National
Arbor Day'.
 Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of
 America in Congress assembled, That the President is hereby authorized and
 requested to issue a proclamation designating the last Friday of April 1991,
 as `National Arbor Day' and calling upon the people of the United States
 to observe such a day with appropriate ceremonies and activities.
Speaker of the House of Representatives.
Vice President of the United States and
President of the Senate.
