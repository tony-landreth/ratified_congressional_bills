H.R.3041
One Hundred Second Congress of the United States of America
AT THE SECOND SESSION
Begun and held at the City of Washington on Friday, the third day of January,
one thousand nine hundred and ninety-two
An Act
To designate the Federal building located at 1520 Market Street, St. Louis,
Missouri,
as the `L. Douglas Abram Federal Building'.
 Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States
 of America in Congress assembled, That the Federal building located at 1520
 Market Street, St. Louis, Missouri, shall hereafter be known and designated as
 the `L. Douglas Abram Federal Building'. Any reference to such building in any
 law, map, regulation, document, record, or other paper of the United States
 shall be deemed to be a reference to the `L. Douglas Abram Federal Building'.
Speaker of the House of Representatives.
Vice President of the United States and
President of the Senate.
