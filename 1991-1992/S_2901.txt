S.2901
One Hundred Second Congress of the United States of America
AT THE SECOND SESSION
Begun and held at the City of Washington on Friday, the third day of January,
one thousand nine hundred and ninety-two
An Act
To direct the Secretary of Health and Human Services to extend the waiver
granted to the Tennessee Primary Care Network of the enrollment mix requirement
under the medicaid program.
 Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United
 States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. EXTENSION OF MEDICAID WAIVER FOR TENNESSEE PRIMARY CARE NETWORK.
 Section 6411(f) of the Omnibus Budget Reconciliation Act of 1989 is amended
 by striking `June 30, 1992' and inserting `January 31, 1994'.
Passed the Senate June 26 (legislative day, June 16), 1992.
Speaker of the House of Representatives.
Vice President of the United States and
President of the Senate.
