S.2905
One Hundred Second Congress of the United States of America
AT THE SECOND SESSION
Begun and held at the City of Washington on Friday, the third day of January,
one thousand nine hundred and ninety-two
An Act
To provide a 4-month extension of the transition rule for separate
capitalization
of savings associations' subsidiaries.
 Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United
 States of America in Congress assembled, That section 5(t)(5)(D)(ii) of
 the Home Owners' Loan Act (12 U.S.C. 1464(t)(5)(D)(ii)) is amended--
 (1) by striking `June 30, 1992' and inserting `October 31, 1992'; and
 (2) by striking `July 1, 1992' and inserting `November 1, 1992'.
Speaker of the House of Representatives.
Vice President of the United States and
President of the Senate.
