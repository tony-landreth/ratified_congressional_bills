S.3327
One Hundred Second Congress of the United States of America
AT THE SECOND SESSION
Begun and held at the City of Washington on Friday, the third day of January,
one thousand nine hundred and ninety-two
An Act
To amend the Agricultural Adjustment Act of 1938 to permit the acre-for-acre
transfer of an acreage allotment or quota for certain commodities, and for
other purposes.
  Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United
  States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. ACRE-FOR-ACRE TRANSFER OF CERTAIN ACREAGE ALLOTMENTS.
  Section 318 of the Agricultural Adjustment Act of 1938 (7 U.S.C. 1314d)
  is amended by striking subsection (e) and inserting the following new
  subsection:
  `(e) The transfer of an allotment or quota under this section shall be
  approved acre for acre.'.
Speaker of the House of Representatives.
Vice President of the United States and
President of the Senate.
