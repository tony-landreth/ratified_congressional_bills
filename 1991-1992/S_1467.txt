S.1467
One Hundred Second Congress of the United States of America
AT THE SECOND SESSION
Begun and held at the City of Washington on Friday, the third day of January,
one thousand nine hundred and ninety-two
An Act
To designate the Federal Building and the United States Courthouse located at
15 Lee Street in Montgomery, Alabama, as the `Frank M. Johnson, Jr. Federal
Building and United States Courthouse'.
 Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United
 States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. DESIGNATION.
 The Federal Building and United States Courthouse located at 15 Lee Street in
 Montgomery, Alabama, shall be known and designated as the `Frank M. Johnson,
 Jr. Federal Building and United States Courthouse'.
SEC. 2. REFERENCES.
 Any reference in any law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record
 of the United States to the Federal Building and United States Courthouse
 referred to in section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference to the `Frank
 M. Johnson, Jr. Federal Building and United States Courthouse'.
Speaker of the House of Representatives.
Vice President of the United States and
President of the Senate.
