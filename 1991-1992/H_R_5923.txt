H.R.5923
One Hundred Second Congress of the United States of America
AT THE SECOND SESSION
Begun and held at the City of Washington on Friday, the third day of January,
one thousand nine hundred and ninety-two
An Act
For the relief of Anna C. Massari.
  Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United
  States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. WAIVER OF TIME LIMITATIONS.
  (a) IN GENERAL- The time limitations set forth in section 3702(b) of title
  31, United States Code, shall not apply with respect to a claim for the
  disbursement of pay due by the Department of the Navy to Anna C. Massari,
  as represented by payroll checks that were issued to, but not negotiated
  by, Anna C. Massari prior to March 31, 1991.
  (b) DEADLINE- Subsection (a) shall apply only if Anna C. Massari or her
  authorized representative submits a claim pursuant to such subsection
  before the expiration of the 6-month period beginning on the date of the
  enactment of this Act.
Speaker of the House of Representatives.
Vice President of the United States and
President of the Senate.
