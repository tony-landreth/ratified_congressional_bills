H.R.5453
One Hundred Second Congress of the United States of America
AT THE SECOND SESSION
Begun and held at the City of Washington on Friday, the third day of January,
one thousand nine hundred and ninety-two
An Act
To designate the Central Square facility of the United States Postal Service
in Cambridge, Massachusetts, as the `Clifton Merriman Post Office Building'.
  Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United
  States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. DESIGNATION.
  The Central Square facility of the United States Postal Service located
  at 770 Massachusetts Avenue in Cambridge, Massachusetts, shall be known
  and designated as the `Clifton Merriman Post Office Building'.
SEC. 2. REFERENCES.
  Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record
  of the United States to the facility referred to in section 1 shall be
  deemed to be a reference to the `Clifton Merriman Post Office Building'.
Speaker of the House of Representatives.
Vice President of the United States and
President of the Senate.
