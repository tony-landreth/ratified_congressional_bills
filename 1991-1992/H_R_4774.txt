H.R.4774
One Hundred Second Congress of the United States of America
AT THE SECOND SESSION
Begun and held at the City of Washington on Friday, the third day of January,
one thousand nine hundred and ninety-two
An Act
To provide flexibility to the Secretary of Agriculture to carry out food
assistance
programs in certain countries.
 Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United
 States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. USE OF FOREIGN CURRENCY PROCEEDS.
 Section 416(b)(7)(D)(iv) of the Agricultural Act of 1949 (7
 U.S.C. 1431(b)(7)(D)(iv)) is amended--
 (1) by striking `and (II)' and inserting `(II)'; and
 (2) by striking the final period and inserting `, and (III) in a country
 other than the country of origin, if such proceeds are generated in a
 currency generally accepted in such other country.'.
Speaker of the House of Representatives.
Vice President of the United States and
President of the Senate.
