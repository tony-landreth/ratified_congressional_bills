H.R.5998
One Hundred Second Congress of the United States of America
AT THE SECOND SESSION
Begun and held at the City of Washington on Friday, the third day of January,
one thousand nine hundred and ninety-two
An Act
For the relief of the Wilkinson County School District, in the State of
Mississippi.
  Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United
  States of America in Congress assembled, That the Secretary of the Treasury
  is authorized and directed to pay, out of any money in the Treasury not
  otherwise appropriated, to the Wilkinson County School District, in the
  State of Mississippi, an amount equal to $57,896 in full satisfaction of
  all claims of such School District relating to any overpayment of windfall
  profit tax for periods ending before January 1, 1983.
Speaker of the House of Representatives.
Vice President of the United States and
President of the Senate.
