 S.807
One Hundred Second Congress of the United States of America
AT THE SECOND SESSION
Begun and held at the City of Washington on Friday, the third day of January,
one thousand nine hundred and ninety-two
An Act
To permit Mount Olivet Cemetery Association of Salt Lake City, Utah, to lease
a certain tract of land for a period of not more than 70 years.
 Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United
 States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. RELEASE OF RESTRICTION.
 Notwithstanding the Act of January 23, 1909 (chapter 37, 35 Stat. 589), the
 Secretary of the Interior shall execute such instruments as may be necessary
 to allow the Mount Olivet Cemetery Association of Salt Lake City, Utah,
 to lease for use other than as a cemetery, for a period of not more than 70
 years, any portion of the land described in the first section of that Act,
 excluding the tract of land granted to Salt Lake City, Utah, pursuant to
 the Act of April 3, 1952 (66 Stat. 36), so long as such additional use will
 not prevent future use for cemetery purposes.
Speaker of the House of Representatives.
Vice President of the United States and
President of the Senate.
