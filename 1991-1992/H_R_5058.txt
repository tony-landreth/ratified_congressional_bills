H.R.5058
One Hundred Second Congress of the United States of America
AT THE SECOND SESSION
Begun and held at the City of Washington on Friday, the third day of January,
one thousand nine hundred and ninety-two
An Act
To authorize appropriations for the American Folklife Center for fiscal
year 1993.
  Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United
  States of America in Congress assembled, That section 8 of the American
  Folklife Preservation Act (20 U.S.C. 2107) is amended--
  (1) by striking out `1991, and' and inserting in lieu thereof `1991,'; and
  (2) by inserting after `1992' the following: `, and $1,120,000 for the
  fiscal year ending September 30, 1993'.
Speaker of the House of Representatives.
Vice President of the United States and
President of the Senate.
