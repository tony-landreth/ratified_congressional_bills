H.J.Res.23
One Hundred Second Congress of the United States of America
AT THE FIRST SESSION
Begun and held at the City of Washington on Thursday, the third day of January,
one thousand nine hundred and ninety-one
Joint Resolution
To authorize the President to issue a proclamation designating each of the
weeks beginning on November 24, 1991, and November 22, 1992, as `National
Family Week'.
 Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of
 America in Congress assembled, That the President is hereby authorized and
 requested to issue a proclamation designating each of the weeks beginning
 on November 24, 1991, and November 22, 1992, as `National Family Week'.
Speaker of the House of Representatives.
Vice President of the United States and
President of the Senate.
