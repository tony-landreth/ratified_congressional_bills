S.2625
One Hundred Second Congress of the United States of America
AT THE SECOND SESSION
Begun and held at the City of Washington on Friday, the third day of January,
one thousand nine hundred and ninety-two
An Act
To designate the United States courthouse being constructed at 400 Cooper
Street
in Camden, New Jersey, as the `Mitchell H. Cohen United States Courthouse'.
  Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United
  States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. DESIGNATION.
  The United States courthouse under construction at 400 Cooper Street in
  Camden, New Jersey, shall be known and designated as the `Mitchell H. Cohen
  United States Courthouse'.
SEC. 2. REFERENCES.
  Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record
  of the United States to the courthouse referred to in section 1 shall be
  deemed to be a reference to the `Mitchell H. Cohen United States Courthouse'.
Speaker of the House of Representatives.
Vice President of the United States and
President of the Senate.
