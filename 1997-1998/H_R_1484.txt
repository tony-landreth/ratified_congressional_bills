Public Law 105-94
105th Congress

                                 An Act


 
  To redesignate the United States courthouse located at 100 Franklin 
    Street in Dublin, Georgia, as the ``J. Roy Rowland United States 
          Courthouse''. <<NOTE: Nov. 19, 1997 -  [H.R. 1484]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. REDESIGNATION.

    The United States courthouse located at 100 Franklin Street in 
Dublin, Georgia, and known as the Dublin Federal Courthouse, shall be 
known and designated as the ``J. Roy Rowland United States Courthouse''.

SEC. 2. REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the United States courthouse referred to 
in section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``J. Roy Rowland 
United States Courthouse''.

    Approved November 19, 1997.
