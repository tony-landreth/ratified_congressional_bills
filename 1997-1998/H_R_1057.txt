Public Law 105-90
105th Congress

                                 An Act


 
  To designate the building in Indianapolis, Indiana, which houses the 
operations of the Indianapolis Main Post Office as the ``Andrew Jacobs, 
   Jr. Post Office Building''. <<NOTE: Nov. 19, 1997 -  [H.R. 1057]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. DESIGNATION.

    The building in Indianapolis, Indiana, which houses the operations 
of the Indianapolis Main Post Office shall be known and designated as 
the ``Andrew Jacobs, Jr. Post Office Building''.

SEC. 2. REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the building referred to in section 1 
shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Andrew Jacobs, Jr. Post 
Office Building''.

    Approved November 19, 1997.
