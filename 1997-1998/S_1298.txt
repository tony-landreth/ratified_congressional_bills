Public Law 105-299
105th Congress

                                 An Act


 
  To designate a Federal building located in Florence, Alabama, as the 
``Justice John McKinley Federal Building''. <<NOTE: Oct. 27, 1998 -  [S. 
                                1298]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. DESIGNATION OF JUSTICE JOHN MCKINLEY FEDERAL BUILDING.

    The Federal building located at 210 North Seminary Street in 
Florence, Alabama, shall be known and designated as the ``Justice John 
McKinley Federal Building''.

SEC. 2. REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the Federal building referred to in 
section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Justice John 
McKinley Federal Building''.

    Approved October 27, 1998.
