                                 An Act


 
  To amend the charter of the Future Farmers of America, and for other 
            purposes. <<NOTE: Feb. 21, 2019 -  [H.R. 439]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled, <<NOTE: National FFA

SECTION 1. <<NOTE: 36 USC 101 note.>> SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``National FFA Organization's Federal 
Charter Amendments Act''.
SEC. 2. ORGANIZATION.

    Section 70901 of title 36, United States Code, is amended--

SEC. 3. PURPOSES OF THE CORPORATION.

    Section 70902 of title 36, United States Code, is amended--

            (6) by inserting before paragraph (7), as redesignated by

            ``(1) to be an integral component of instruction in 
        agricultural education, including instruction relating to

SEC. 5. GOVERNING BODY.

    Section 70904 of title 36, United States Code, is amended--

                          ``(i) the Secretary of Education, or the

                      ``bylaws'' and inserting ``constitution or bylaws

    ``(b) Officers.--The officers of the FFA, the terms of officers, and 
the election of officers, are as provided in the constitution or bylaws 
of the FFA, except that such officers shall include--
            ``(1) a national advisor;
            ``(2) an executive secretary; and
            ``(3) a treasurer.

    ``(c) Governing Committee.--
            ``(1) The board may designate a governing committee. The 
        terms and method of selecting the governing committee members 
        are as provided in the constitution or bylaws of the FFA, except 
        that all members of the governing committee shall be members of 
        the board of directors and at all times the governing committee 
        shall be comprised of not less than 3 individuals.
            ``(2) When the board is not in session, the governing 
        committee has the powers of the board subject to the board's 
        direction and may authorize the seal of the FFA to be affixed to 
        all papers that require it.
            ``(3) The board shall designate to such committee--
                    ``(A) the chair of the board;
                    ``(B) the executive secretary of the board; and
                    ``(C) the treasurer of the board.''.

SEC. 10. RELATIONSHIP TO FEDERAL AGENCIES.

    Section 70909 of title 36, United States Code, is amended to read as 
follows:
``SEC. 70909. RELATIONSHIP TO FEDERAL AGENCIES.

    ``(a) In General.--On request of the board of directors, the FFA may 
collaborate with Federal agencies, including the Department of Education 
and the Department of Agriculture on matters of mutual interest and 
benefit.
    ``(b) Agency Assistance.--Those Federal agencies may make personnel, 
services, and facilities available to administer or assist in the 
administration of the activities of the FFA.

[[Page 133 STAT. 482]]

    ``(c) Agency Compensation.--Personnel of the Federal agencies may 
not receive compensation from the FFA for their services, except that 
travel and other legitimate expenses as defined by the Federal agencies 
and approved by the board may be paid.
    ``(d) Cooperation With State Boards.--The Federal agencies also may 
cooperate with State boards and other organizations for career and 
technical education to assist in the promotion of activities of the 
FFA.''.
