Public Law 116-118
116th Congress

                            Joint Resolution


 
Providing for the reappointment of John Fahey as a citizen regent of the 
    Board of Regents of the Smithsonian Institution. <<NOTE: Mar. 2, 
                        2020 -  [S.J. Res. 65]>> 

    Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United 
States of America in Congress assembled, That, in accordance with 
section 5581 of the Revised Statutes (20 U.S.C. 43), the vacancy on the 
Board of Regents of the Smithsonian Institution, in the class other than 
Members of Congress, occurring by reason of the expiration of the term 
of John Fahey of Massachusetts on February 20, 2020, is filled by the 
reappointment of the incumbent. The <<NOTE: Time period. Effective 
date.>>  reappointment is for a term of six years, beginning on the 
later of February 20, 2020, or the date of the enactment of this joint 
resolution.

    Approved March 2, 2020.
