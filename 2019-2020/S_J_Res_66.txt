Public Law 116-138
116th Congress

                            Joint Resolution


 
 Providing for the appointment of Denise O'Leary as a citizen regent of 
 the Board of Regents of the Smithsonian Institution. <<NOTE: Apr. 10, 
                        2020 -  [S.J. Res. 66]>> 

    Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United 
States of America in Congress assembled, That, in accordance with 
section 5581 of the Revised Statutes (20 U.S.C. 43), the vacancy on the 
Board of Regents of the Smithsonian Institution, in the class other than 
Members of Congress, occurring by reason of the resignation of Barbara 
M. Barrett of Arizona on October 17, 2019, is filled by the appointment 
of Denise O'Leary of Colorado. The appointment <<NOTE: Time 
period. Effective date.>>  is for a term of six years, beginning on the 
date of the enactment of this joint resolution

    Approved April 10, 2020.
