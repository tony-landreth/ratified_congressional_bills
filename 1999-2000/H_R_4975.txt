Public Law 106-347
106th Congress

                                 An Act


 
To designate the post office and courthouse located at 2 Federal Square, 
    Newark, New Jersey, as the ``Frank R. Lautenberg Post Office and 
          Courthouse''. <<NOTE: Oct. 23, 2000 -  [H.R. 4975]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. DESIGNATION.

    The post office and courthouse located at 2 Federal Square, Newark, 
New Jersey, shall be known and designated as the ``Frank R. Lautenberg 
Post Office and Courthouse''.

SEC. 2. REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the post office and courthouse referred 
to in section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Frank R. 
Lautenberg Post Office and Courthouse''.

    Approved October 23, 2000.
