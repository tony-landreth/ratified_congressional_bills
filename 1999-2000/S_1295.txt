Public Law 106-289
106th Congress

                                 An Act


 
 To designate the United States Post Office located at 3813 Main Street 
  in East Chicago, Indiana, as the ``Lance Corporal Harold Gomez Post 
             Office''. <<NOTE: Oct. 10, 2000 -  [S. 1295]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. DESIGNATION OF LANCE CORPORAL HAROLD GOMEZ POST OFFICE.

    The United States Post Office located at 3813 Main Street in East 
Chicago, Indiana, shall be known and designated as the ``Lance Corporal 
Harold Gomez Post Office''.

SEC. 2. REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the post office referred to in section 1 
shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Lance Corporal Harold Gomez 
Post Office''.

    Approved October 10, 2000.
