Public Law 106-198
106th Congress

                            Joint Resolution


 
 Providing for the appointment of Alan G. Spoon as a citizen regent of 
  the Board of Regents of the Smithsonian Institution. <<NOTE: May 5, 
                        2000 -  [S.J. Res. 40]>> 

    Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United 
States of America in Congress assembled, That, in accordance with 
section 5581 of the Revised Statutes of the United States (20 U.S.C. 
43), the vacancy on the Board of Regents of the Smithsonian Institution, 
in the class other than Members of Congress, occurring by reason of 
resignation of Louis Gerstner of New York, is filled by the appointment 
of Alan G. Spoon of Maryland. <<NOTE: Effective date.>>  The appointment 
is for a term of 6 years and shall take effect on the date of enactment 
of this joint resolution.

    Approved May 5, 2000.
