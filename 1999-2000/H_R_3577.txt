Public Law 106-371
106th Congress

                                 An Act


 
 To increase the amount authorized to be <<NOTE: Oct. 27, 2000 -  [H.R. 
3577]>> appropriated for the north side pumping division of the Minidoka 
                       reclamation project, Idaho.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled, That section 5 of the 
Act of September 30, 1950 (chapter 1114; 64 Stat. 1085), authorizing 
appropriations for the north side pumping division of the Minidoka 
reclamation project, Idaho, is amended by striking ``$11,395,000'' and 
inserting ``$14,200,000''.

    Approved October 27, 2000.
