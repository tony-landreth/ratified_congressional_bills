Public Law 106-28
106th Congress

                                 An Act


 
 To designate the United States courthouse <<NOTE: May 13, 1999 -  [S. 
460]>>  located at 401 South Michigan Street in South Bend, Indiana, as 
    the ``Robert K. Rodibaugh United States Bankruptcy Courthouse''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. DESIGNATION OF ROBERT K. RODIBAUGH UNITED STATES 
                            BANKRUPTCY COURTHOUSE.

    The United States courthouse located at 401 South Michigan Street in 
South Bend, Indiana, shall be known and designated as the ``Robert K. 
Rodibaugh United States Bankruptcy Courthouse''.

SEC. 2. REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the United States courthouse referred to 
in section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Robert K. 
Rodibaugh United States Bankruptcy Courthouse''.

    Approved May 13, 1999.
