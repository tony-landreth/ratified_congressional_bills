Public Law 106-308
106th Congress

                                 An Act


 
   To designate the Federal courthouse at 145 East Simpson Avenue in 
         Jackson, Wyoming, as the ``Clifford P. Hansen Federal 
           Courthouse''. <<NOTE: Oct. 13, 2000 -  [S. 1794]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. DESIGNATION OF CLIFFORD P. HANSEN FEDERAL COURTHOUSE.

    The Federal courthouse at 145 East Simpson Avenue in Jackson, 
Wyoming, shall be known and designated as the ``Clifford P. Hansen 
Federal Courthouse''.

SEC. 2. REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the Federal courthouse referred to in 
section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Clifford P. Hansen 
Federal Courthouse''.

    Approved October 13, 2000.
