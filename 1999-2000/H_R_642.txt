Public Law 106-231
106th Congress

                                 An Act


 
To redesignate the Federal building located at 701 South Santa Fe Avenue 
 in Compton, California, and known as the Compton Main Post Office, as 
  the ``Mervyn Malcolm Dymally Post Office Building''. <<NOTE: July 6, 
                          2000 -  [H.R. 642]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. REDESIGNATION.

    The Federal building located at 701 South Santa Fe Avenue in 
Compton, California, and known as the Compton Main Post Office, shall be 
known and designated as the ``Mervyn Malcolm Dymally Post Office 
Building''.

SEC. 2. REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the Federal building referred to in 
section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Mervyn Malcolm 
Dymally Post Office Building''.

    Approved July 6, 2000.
