Private Law 106-24
106th Congress

                                 An Act


 
   For the relief of Jose Guadalupe Tellez Pinales. <<NOTE: Nov. 22, 
                          2000 -  [S. 2289]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. PERMANENT RESIDENCE.

    Notwithstanding any other provision of law, for purposes of the 
Immigration and Nationality Act (8 U.S.C. 1101 et seq.), Jose Guadalupe 
Tellez Pinales shall be held and considered to have been lawfully 
admitted to the United States for permanent residence as of the date of 
the enactment of this Act upon payment of the required visa fee.

    Approved November 22, 2000.
