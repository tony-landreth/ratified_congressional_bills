Public Law 106-130
106th Congress

                                 An Act


 
To provide for the holding of court at Natchez, Mississippi, in the same 
    manner as court is held at Vicksburg, Mississippi, and for other 
             purposes. <<NOTE: Dec. 6, 1999 -  [S. 1418]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. HOLDING OF COURT AT NATCHEZ, MISSISSIPPI.

    Section 104(b)(3) of title 28, United States Code, is amended in the 
second sentence by striking all beginning with the colon through 
``United States''.

SEC. 2. HOLDING OF COURT AT WHEATON, ILLINOIS.

    Section 93(a)(1) of title 28, United States Code, is amended by 
adding after Chicago ``and Wheaton''.

    Approved December 6, 1999.
