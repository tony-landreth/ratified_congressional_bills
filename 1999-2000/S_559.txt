Public Law 106-72
106th Congress

                                 An Act


 
 To <<NOTE: Oct. 19, 1999 -  [S. 559]>> designate the Federal building 
 located at 300 East 8th Street in Austin, Texas, as the ``J.J. `Jake' 
                       Pickle Federal Building''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. DESIGNATION.

    The Federal Building located at 300 East 8th Street in Austin, 
Texas, shall be known and designated as the ``J.J. `Jake' Pickle Federal 
Building''.

SEC. 2. REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the Federal building referred to in 
section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``J.J. `Jake' Pickle 
Federal Building''.

    Approved October 19, 1999.
