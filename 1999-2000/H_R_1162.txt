Public Law 106-295
106th Congress

                                 An Act


 
   To <<NOTE: Oct. 13, 2000 -  [H.R. 1162]>>  designate the bridge on 
   United States Route 231 that crosses the Ohio River between Maceo, 
 Kentucky, and Rockport, Indiana, as the ``William H. Natcher Bridge''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. DESIGNATION.

    The bridge on United States Route 231 that crosses the Ohio River 
between Maceo, Kentucky, and Rockport, Indiana, shall be known and 
designated as the ``William H. Natcher Bridge''.

SEC. 2. REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the bridge referred to in section 1 shall 
be deemed to be a reference to the ``William H. Natcher Bridge''.

    Approved October 13, 2000.
