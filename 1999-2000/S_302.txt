Private Law 106-7
106th Congress

                                 An Act


 
For the relief of Kerantha Poole-Christian. <<NOTE: Oct. 13, 2000 -  [S. 
                                 302]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. CLASSIFICATION AS A CHILD UNDER THE IMMIGRATION AND 
                            NATIONALITY ACT.

    (a) <<NOTE: Clifton Christian. Linette Christian.>>  In General.--In 
the administration of the Immigration and Nationality Act, Kerantha 
Poole-Christian shall be classified as a child within the meaning of 
section 101(b)(1)(E) of such Act, upon approval of a petition filed on 
her behalf by Clifton or Linette Christian, citizens of the United 
States, pursuant to section 204 of such Act.

    (b) Limitation.--No natural parent, brother, or sister, if any, of 
Kerantha Poole-Christian shall, by virtue of such relationship, be 
accorded any right, privilege, or status under the Immigration and 
Nationality Act.

    Approved October 13, 2000.
