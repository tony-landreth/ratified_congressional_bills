Public Law 106-196
106th Congress

                                 An Act


 
To designate the United States courthouse located at 223 Broad Avenue in 
           Albany, Georgia, as the ``C.B. King United States 
            Courthouse''. <<NOTE: May 2, 2000 -  [S. 1567]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. DESIGNATION.

    The United States courthouse located at 223 Broad Avenue in Albany, 
Georgia, shall be known and designated as the ``C.B. King United States 
Courthouse''.

SEC. 2. REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the United States courthouse referred to 
in section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``C.B. King United 
States Courthouse''.

    Approved May 2, 2000.
