Public Law 107-146
107th Congress

                                 An Act


 
To designate the United States Post Office located at 60 Third Avenue in 
         Long Branch, New Jersey, as the ``Pat King Post Office 
            Building''. <<NOTE: Feb. 14, 2002 -  [S. 1026]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. DESIGNATION OF PAT KING POST OFFICE BUILDING.

    The United States Post Office located at 60 Third Avenue in Long 
Branch, New Jersey, shall be known and designated as the ``Pat King Post 
Office Building''.

SEC. 2. REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the United States Post Office referred to 
in section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference to the Pat King Post 
Office Building.

    Approved February 14, 2002.
