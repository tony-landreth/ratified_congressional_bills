Public Law 107-101
107th Congress

                            Joint Resolution


 
  Providing for the appointment of Patricia Q. Stonesifer as a citizen 
           regent of the Board of Regents of the Smithsonian 
         Institution. <<NOTE: Dec. 21, 2001 -  [S.J. Res. 26]>> 

    Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United 
States of America in Congress assembled, That, in accordance with 
section 5581 of the Revised Statutes of the United States (20 U.S.C. 
43), the vacancy on the Board of Regents of the Smithsonian Institution, 
in the class other than Members of Congress, occurring by reason of the 
expiration of the term of Dr. Homer Neal of Michigan on December 7, 
2001, is filled by the appointment of Patricia Q. Stonesifer of 
Washington. <<NOTE: Effective date.>> The appointment is for a term of 6 
years and shall take effect on December 8, 2001.

    Approved December 21, 2001.
