Public Law 107-291
107th Congress

                                 An Act


 
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 
   206 South Main Street in Glennville, Georgia, as the ``Michael Lee 
     Woodcock Post Office''. <<NOTE: Nov. 7, 2002 -  [H.R. 5574]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. DESIGNATION.

    The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 206 
South Main Street in Glennville, Georgia, shall be known and designated 
as the ``Michael Lee Woodcock Post Office''.

SEC. 2. REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the facility referred to in section 1 
shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Michael Lee Woodcock Post 
Office''.

    Approved November 7, 2002.
