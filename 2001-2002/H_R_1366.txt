Public Law 107-190
107th Congress

                                 An Act


 
To designate the United States Post Office building located at 3101 West 
 Sunflower Avenue in Santa Ana, California, as the ``Hector G. Godinez 
     Post Office Building''. <<NOTE: June 18, 2002 -  [H.R. 1366]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. DESIGNATION.

    The United States Post Office building located at 3101 West 
Sunflower Avenue in Santa Ana, California, shall be known and designated 
as the ``Hector G. Godinez Post Office Building''.

SEC. 2. REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, regulation, map, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the United States Post Office building 
referred to in section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference to the 
``Hector G. Godinez Post Office Building''.

    Approved June 18, 2002.
