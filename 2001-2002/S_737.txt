Public Law 107-144
107th Congress

                                 An Act


 
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 
811 South Main Street in Yerington, Nevada, as the ``Joseph E. Dini, Jr. 
           Post Office''. <<NOTE: Feb. 14, 2002 -  [S. 737]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. JOSEPH E. DINI, JR. POST OFFICE.

    (a) Designation.--The facility of the United States Postal Service 
located at 811 South Main Street in Yerington, Nevada, shall be known 
and designated as the ``Joseph E. Dini, Jr. Post Office''.
    (b) References.--Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, 
paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to 
in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Joseph E. 
Dini, Jr. Post Office.

    Approved February 14, 2002.
