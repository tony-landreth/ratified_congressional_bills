Public Law 112-251
112th Congress

                                 An Act


 
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 
19 East Merced Street in Fowler, California, as the ``Cecil E. Bolt Post 
            Office''. <<NOTE: Jan. 10, 2013 -  [H.R. 4389]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. CECIL E. BOLT POST OFFICE.

    (a) Designation.--The facility of the United States Postal Service 
located at 19 East Merced Street in Fowler, California, shall be known 
and designated as the ``Cecil E. Bolt Post Office''.
    (b) References.--Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, 
paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to 
in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Cecil E. 
Bolt Post Office''.

    Approved January 10, 2013.
