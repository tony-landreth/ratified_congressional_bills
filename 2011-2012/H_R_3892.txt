Public Law 112-247
112th Congress

                                 An Act


 
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 
    8771 Auburn Folsom Road in Roseville, California, as the ``Lance 
  Corporal Victor A. Dew Post Office''. <<NOTE: Jan. 10, 2013 -  [H.R. 
                                3892]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. LANCE CORPORAL VICTOR A. DEW POST OFFICE.

    (a) Designation.--The facility of the United States Postal Service 
located at 8771 Auburn Folsom Road in Roseville, California, shall be 
known and designated as the ``Lance Corporal Victor A. Dew Post 
Office''.
    (b) References.--Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, 
paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to 
in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Lance 
Corporal Victor A. Dew Post Office''.

    Approved January 10, 2013.
