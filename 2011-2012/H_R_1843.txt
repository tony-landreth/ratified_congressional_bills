Public Law 112-47
112th Congress

                                 An Act


 
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 
489 Army Drive in Barrigada, Guam, as the ``John Pangelinan Gerber Post 
        Office Building''. <<NOTE: Nov. 7, 2011 -  [H.R. 1843]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. JOHN PANGELINAN GERBER POST OFFICE BUILDING.

    (a) Designation.--The facility of the United States Postal Service 
located at 489 Army Drive in Barrigada, Guam, shall be known and 
designated as the ``John Pangelinan Gerber Post Office Building''.
    (b) References.--Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, 
paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to 
in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``John 
Pangelinan Gerber Post Office Building''.

    Approved November 7, 2011.
