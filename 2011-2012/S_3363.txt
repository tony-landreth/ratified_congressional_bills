Public Law 112-169
112th Congress

                                 An Act


 
 To provide for the use of National Infantry Museum and Soldier Center 
Commemorative Coin surcharges, and for other purposes. <<NOTE: Aug. 10, 
                          2012 -  [S. 3363]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. NATIONAL INFANTRY MUSEUM AND SOLDIER CENTER 
                              COMMEMORATIVE COIN SURCHARGES.

    Section 6(b) of the National Infantry Museum and Soldier Center 
Commemorative Coin Act (Public Law 110-357, 122 Stat. 3999) is 
amended <<NOTE: 31 USC 5112 note.>>  by inserting before the period at 
the end the following: ``, and for the retirement of debt associated 
with building the existing National Infantry Museum and Soldier 
Center''.

    Approved August 10, 2012.
