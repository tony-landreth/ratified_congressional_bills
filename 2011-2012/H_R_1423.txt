Public Law 112-108
112th Congress

                                 An Act


 
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 
   115 4th Avenue Southwest in Ardmore, Oklahoma, as the ``Specialist 
    Micheal E. Phillips Post Office''. <<NOTE: May 15, 2012 -  [H.R. 
                                1423]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. SPECIALIST MICHEAL E. PHILLIPS POST OFFICE.

    (a) Designation.--The facility of the United States Postal Service 
located at 115 4th Avenue Southwest in Ardmore, Oklahoma, shall be known 
and designated as the ``Specialist Micheal E. Phillips Post Office''.
    (b) References.--Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, 
paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to 
in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Specialist 
Micheal E. Phillips Post Office''.

    Approved May 15, 2012.
