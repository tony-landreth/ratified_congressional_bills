Public Law 112-19
112th Congress

                            Joint Resolution


 
  Providing for the reappointment of Shirley Ann Jackson as a citizen 
           regent of the Board of Regents of the Smithsonian 
         Institution. <<NOTE: June 24, 2011 -  [S.J. Res. 7]>> 

    Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United 
States of America in Congress assembled, <<NOTE: Effective date.>> That, 
in accordance with section 5581 of the Revised Statutes (20 U.S.C. 43), 
the vacancy on the Board of Regents of the Smithsonian Institution, in 
the class other than Members of Congress, occurring by reason of the 
expiration of the term of Shirley Ann Jackson of New York, is filled by 
reappointment of the incumbent for a term of 6 years, effective May 6, 
2011.

    Approved June 24, 2011.
