Public Law 115-28
115th Congress

                            Joint Resolution


 
 Providing for the appointment of Michael Govan as a citizen regent of 
 the Board of Regents of the Smithsonian Institution. <<NOTE: Apr. 19, 
                        2017 -  [S.J. Res. 35]>> 

    Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United 
States of America in Congress assembled, That, in accordance with 
section 5581 of the Revised Statutes (20 U.S.C. 43), the vacancy of the 
Board of Regents of the Smithsonian Institution, in the class other than 
Members of Congress, occurring by reason of the expiration of the term 
of Dr. Shirley Ann Jackson of New York on May 5, 2017, is filled by the 
appointment of Michael Govan of California. <<NOTE: Effective date.>>  
The appointment is for a term of 6 years, beginning on May 6, 2017, or 
the date of the enactment of this joint resolution, whichever occurs 
later.

    Approved April 19, 2017.
