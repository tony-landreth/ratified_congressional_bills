Public Law 115-74
115th Congress

                            Joint Resolution


 
  Providing for congressional disapproval under chapter 8 of title 5, 
    United States Code, of the rule submitted by Bureau of Consumer 
             Financial Protection relating to ``Arbitration 
        Agreements''. <<NOTE: Nov. 1, 2017 -  [H.J. Res. 111]>> 

    Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United 
States of America in Congress assembled, That Congress disapproves the 
rule submitted by the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection relating 
to ``Arbitration Agreements'' (82 Fed. Reg. 33210 (July 19, 2017)), and 
such rule shall have no force or effect.

    Approved November 1, 2017.
