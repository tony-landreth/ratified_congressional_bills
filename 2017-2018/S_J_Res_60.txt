Public Law 115-199
115th Congress

                            Joint Resolution


 
   Providing for the reappointment of Barbara M. Barrett as a citizen 
           regent of the Board of Regents of the Smithsonian 
         Institution. <<NOTE: July 20, 2018 -  [S.J. Res. 60]>> 

    Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United 
States of America in Congress assembled, That, in accordance with 
section 5581 of the Revised Statutes (20 U.S.C. 43), the vacancy on the 
Board of Regents of the Smithsonian Institution, in the class other than 
Members of Congress, occurring by reason of the expiration of the term 
of Barbara M. Barrett of Arizona on January 10, 2019, is filled by the 
reappointment of the incumbent. <<NOTE: Time period. Effective date.>>  
The reappointment is for a term of 6 years, beginning on the later of 
January 11, 2019, or the date of the enactment of this joint resolution.

    Approved July 20, 2018.
