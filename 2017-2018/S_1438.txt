Public Law 115-128
115th Congress

                                 An Act


 
To redesignate the Jefferson National Expansion Memorial in the State of 
    Missouri as the ``Gateway Arch National Park''. <<NOTE: Feb. 22, 
                          2018 -  [S. 1438]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled, <<NOTE: Gateway Arch 
National Park Designation Act. 16 USC 450jj note.>> 
SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Gateway Arch National Park 
Designation Act''.
SEC. 2. <<NOTE: 16 USC 450jj-10.>>  DESIGNATION OF GATEWAY ARCH 
                    NATIONAL PARK.

    (a) Redesignation.--The Jefferson National Expansion Memorial 
established under the Act of May 17, 1954 (16 U.S.C. 450jj et seq.), 
shall be known and designated as the ``Gateway Arch National Park''.
    (b) References.--Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, 
paper, or other record of the United States to the Jefferson National 
Expansion Memorial shall be considered to be a reference to the 
``Gateway Arch National Park''.

    Approved February 22, 2018.
