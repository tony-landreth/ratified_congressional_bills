Public Law 115-111
115th Congress

                                 An Act


 
  To facilitate the addition of park administration at the Coltsville 
   National Historical Park, and for other purposes. <<NOTE: Jan. 10, 
                          2018 -  [H.R. 863]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. AMENDMENT TO COLTSVILLE NATIONAL HISTORICAL PARK 
                              DONATION SITE.

    Section 3032(b)(2)(B) of Public Law 113-291 (16 U.S.C. 410qqq) is 
amended by striking ``East Armory'' and inserting ``Colt Armory 
Complex''.

    Approved January 10, 2018.
