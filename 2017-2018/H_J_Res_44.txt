Public Law 115-12
115th Congress

                            Joint Resolution


 
   Disapproving the rule submitted by the Department of the Interior 
  relating to Bureau of Land Management regulations that establish the 
procedures used to prepare, revise, or amend land use plans pursuant to 
  the Federal Land Policy and Management Act of 1976. <<NOTE: Mar. 27, 
                        2017 -  [H.J. Res. 44]>> 

    Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United 
States of America in Congress assembled, That Congress disapproves the 
rule submitted by the Bureau of Land Management of the Department of the 
Interior relating to ``Resource Management Planning'' (published at 81 
Fed. Reg. 89580 (December 12, 2016)), and such rule shall have no force 
or effect.

    Approved March 27, 2017.
