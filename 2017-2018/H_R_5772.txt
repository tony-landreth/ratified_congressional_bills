Public Law 115-242
115th Congress

                                 An Act


 
  To designate the J. Marvin Jones Federal Building and Courthouse in 
Amarillo, Texas, as the ``J. Marvin Jones Federal Building and Mary Lou 
  Robinson United States Courthouse''. <<NOTE: Sept. 18, 2018 -  [H.R. 
                                5772]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. DESIGNATION.

    The J. Marvin Jones Federal Building and Courthouse located at 205 
SE 5th Ave., Amarillo, Texas, shall be known and designated as the ``J. 
Marvin Jones Federal Building and Mary Lou Robinson United States 
Courthouse''.
SEC. 2. REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the Federal building and United States 
courthouse referred to in section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference to 
the ``J. Marvin Jones Federal Building and Mary Lou Robinson United 
States Courthouse''.

    Approved September 18, 2018.
