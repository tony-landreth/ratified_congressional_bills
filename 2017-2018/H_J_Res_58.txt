Public Law 115-14
115th Congress

                            Joint Resolution


 
  Providing for congressional disapproval under chapter 8 of title 5, 
United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Department of Education 
 relating to teacher preparation issues. <<NOTE: Mar. 27, 2017 -  [H.J. 
                               Res. 58]>> 

    Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United 
States of America in Congress assembled, That Congress disapproves the 
rule submitted by the Department of Education relating to teacher 
preparation issues (published at 81 Fed. Reg. 75494 (October 31, 2016)), 
and such rule shall have no force or effect.

    Approved March 27, 2017.
