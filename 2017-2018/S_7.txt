Public Law 115-403
115th Congress

                                 An Act


 
 To amend title 51, United States Code, to extend the authority of the 
 National Aeronautics and Space Administration to enter into leases of 
non-excess property of the Administration. <<NOTE: Dec. 31, 2018 -  [S. 
                                  7]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled, <<NOTE: NASA Enhanced 
Use Leasing Extension Act of 2018. 51 USC 10101 note.>> 
SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``NASA Enhanced Use Leasing Extension 
Act of 2018''.
SEC. 2. EXTENSION OF AUTHORITY TO ENTER INTO LEASES OF NON-EXCESS 
                    PROPERTY OF THE NATIONAL AERONAUTICS AND SPACE 
                    ADMINISTRATION.

    Section 20145(g) of title 51, United States Code, is amended by 
striking ``December 31, 2018'' and inserting ``December 31, 2019''.

    Approved December 31, 2018.
