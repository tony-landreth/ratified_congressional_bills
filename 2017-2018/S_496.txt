Public Law 115-33
115th Congress

                                 An Act


 
To repeal the rule issued by the Federal Highway Administration and the 
    Federal Transit Administration entitled ``Metropolitan Planning 
 Organization Coordination and Planning Area Reform''. <<NOTE: May 12, 
                           2017 -  [S. 496]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. <<NOTE: Applicability.>>  REPEAL.

    The rule issued by the Federal Highway Administration and the 
Federal Transit Administration entitled ``Metropolitan Planning 
Organization Coordination and Planning Area Reform'' (81 Fed. Reg. 93448 
(December 20, 2016)) shall have no force or effect, and any regulation 
revised by that rule shall be applied as if that rule had not been 
issued.

    Approved May 12, 2017.
