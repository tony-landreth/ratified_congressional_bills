Public Law 115-27
115th Congress

                            Joint Resolution


 
Providing for the reappointment of Steve Case as a citizen regent of the 
   Board of Regents of the Smithsonian Institution. <<NOTE: Apr. 19, 
                        2017 -  [S.J. Res. 30]>> 

    Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United 
States of America in Congress assembled, That, in accordance with 
section 5581 of the Revised Statutes (20 U.S.C. 43), the vacancy on the 
Board of Regents of the Smithsonian Institution, in the class other than 
Members of Congress, occurring by reason of the expiration of the term 
of Steve Case of Virginia on April 25, 2017, is filled by the 
reappointment of the incumbent. <<NOTE: Effective date.>>  The 
reappointment is for a term of 6 years, beginning on the later of April 
26, 2017, or the date of the enactment of this joint resolution.

    Approved April 19, 2017.
