Public Law 104-83
104th Congress

                            Joint Resolution


 
Providing for the appointment of Anne D'Harnoncourt as a citizen regent 
of the Board of Regents of the Smithsonian Institution. <<NOTE: Dec. 28, 
                       1995 -  [H.J. Res. 111]>> 

    Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United 
States of America in Congress assembled, That, in accordance with 
section 5581 of the Revised Statutes of the United States (20 U.S.C. 
43), the vacancy on the Board of Regents of the Smithsonian Institution, 
in the class other than Members of Congress, occurring by reason of the 
expiration of the term of Samuel Curtis Johnson of Wisconsin on December 
4, 1995, is filled by the appointment of Anne D'Harnoncourt of 
Pennsylvania. The appointment is for a term of six years and shall take 
effect on December 5, 1995.

    Approved December 28, 1995.
