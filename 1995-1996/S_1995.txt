Public Law 104-222
104th Congress

                                 An Act


 
 To authorize construction of the Smithsonian Institution National Air 
   and Space Museum Dulles Center at Washington Dulles International 
  Airport, and for other purposes. <<NOTE: Oct. 1, 1996 -  [S. 1995]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled, <<NOTE: Virginia.>> 

SECTION 1. <<NOTE: 20 USC 77 note.>>  CONSTRUCTION OF MUSEUM CENTER.

    The Board of Regents of the Smithsonian Institution is authorized to 
construct the Smithsonian Institution National Air and Space Museum 
Dulles Center at Washington Dulles International Airport.

SEC. 2. <<NOTE: 20 USC 77 note.>>  LIMITATION ON USE OF FUNDS.

    No appropriated funds may be used to pay any expense of the 
construction authorized by section 1.

    Approved October 1, 1996.
