Public Law 104-157
104th Congress

                                 An Act


 
To designate the United States Post Office building located at 102 South 
     McLean, Lincoln, Illinois, as the ``Edward Madigan Post Office 
           Building''. <<NOTE: July 9, 1996 -  [H.R. 1880]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. DESIGNATION.

    The United States Post Office building located at 102 South McLean, 
Lincoln, Illinois, shall be known and designated as the ``Edward Madigan 
Post Office Building''.

SEC. 2. REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the United States Post Office building 
referred to in section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference to the 
``Edward Madigan Post Office Building''.

    Approved July 9, 1996.
