Public Law 104-108
104th Congress

                                 An Act


 
   To designate the Federal building located at 1221 Nevin Avenue in 
           Richmond, California, as the ``Frank Hagel Federal 
           Building''. <<NOTE: Feb. 12, 1996 -  [H.R. 2111]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. DESIGNATION.

    The Federal building located at 1221 Nevin Avenue in Richmond, 
California, shall be known and designated as the ``Frank Hagel Federal 
Building''.

SEC. 2. REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the Federal building referred to in 
section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Frank Hagel Federal 
Building''.

    Approved February 12, 1996.
