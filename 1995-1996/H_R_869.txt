Public Law 104-136
104th Congress

                                 An Act


 
 To designate the Federal building and United States courthouse located 
  at 125 Market Street in Youngstown, Ohio, as the ``Thomas D. Lambros 
   Federal Building and United States Courthouse''. <<NOTE: Apr. 30, 
                          1996 -  [H.R. 869]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. DESIGNATION.

    The Federal building and United States courthouse located at 125 
Market Street in Youngstown, Ohio, shall be known and designated as the 
``Thomas D. Lambros Federal Building and United States Courthouse''.

SEC. 2. REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the Federal building and United States 
courthouse referred to in section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference to 
the ``Thomas D. Lambros Federal Building and United States Courthouse''.

    Approved April 30, 1996.
