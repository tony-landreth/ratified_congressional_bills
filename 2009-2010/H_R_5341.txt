Public Law 111-277
111th Congress

                                 An Act


 
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 
  100 Orndorf Drive in Brighton, Michigan, as the ``Joyce Rogers Post 
       Office Building''. <<NOTE: Oct. 13, 2010 -  [H.R. 5341]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. JOYCE ROGERS POST OFFICE BUILDING.

    (a) Designation.--The facility of the United States Postal Service 
located at 100 Orndorf Drive in Brighton, Michigan, shall be known and 
designated as the ``Joyce Rogers Post Office Building''.
    (b) References.--Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, 
paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to 
in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Joyce 
Rogers Post Office Building''.

    Approved October 13, 2010.
