Public Law 111-215
111th Congress

                                 An Act


 
   To modify the date on which the Administrator of the Environmental 
    Protection Agency and applicable States may require permits for 
 discharges from certain vessels. <<NOTE: July 30, 2010 -  [S. 3372]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. DISCHARGES INCIDENTAL TO NORMAL OPERATION OF VESSELS.

    Section 2(a) of Public Law 110-299 (33 U.S.C. 1342 note) is amended 
by striking ``during the 2-year period beginning on the date of 
enactment of this Act'' and inserting ``during the period beginning on 
the date of the enactment of this Act and ending on December 18, 2013''.

    Approved July 30, 2010.
