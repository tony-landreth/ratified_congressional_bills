Public Law 111-75
111th Congress

                                 An Act


 
To designate the United States courthouse located at 525 Magoffin Avenue 
   in El Paso, Texas, as the ``Albert Armendariz, Sr., United States 
          Courthouse''. <<NOTE: Oct. 19, 2009 -  [H.R. 2053]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. DESIGNATION.

    The United States courthouse located at 525 Magoffin Avenue in El 
Paso, Texas, shall be known and designated as the ``Albert Armendariz, 
Sr., United States Courthouse''.
SEC. 2. REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the United States courthouse referred to 
in section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Albert 
Armendariz, Sr., United States Courthouse''.

    Approved October 19, 2009.
