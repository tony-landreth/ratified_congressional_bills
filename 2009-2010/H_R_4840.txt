Public Law 111-208
111th Congress

                                 An Act


 
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 
 1981 Cleveland Avenue in Columbus, Ohio, as the ``Clarence D. Lumpkin 
         Post Office''. <<NOTE: July 27, 2010 -  [H.R. 4840]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. CLARENCE D. LUMPKIN POST OFFICE.

    (a) Designation.--The facility of the United States Postal Service 
located at 1981 Cleveland Avenue in Columbus, Ohio, shall be known and 
designated as the ``Clarence D. Lumpkin Post Office''.
    (b) References.--Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, 
paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to 
in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Clarence D. 
Lumpkin Post Office''.

    Approved July 27, 2010.
