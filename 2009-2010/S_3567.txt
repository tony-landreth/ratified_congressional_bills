Public Law 111-288
111th Congress

                                 An Act


 
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 
 100 Broadway in Lynbrook, New York, as the ``Navy Corpsman Jeffrey L. 
  Wiener Post Office Building''. <<NOTE: Nov. 30, 2010 -  [S. 3567]>> 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. NAVY CORPSMAN JEFFREY L. WIENER POST OFFICE BUILDING.

    (a) Designation.--The facility of the United States Postal Service 
located at 100 Broadway in Lynbrook, New York, shall be known and 
designated as the ``Navy Corpsman Jeffrey L. Wiener Post Office 
Building''.
    (b) References.--Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, 
paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to 
in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Navy 
Corpsman Jeffrey L. Wiener Post Office Building''.

    Approved November 30, 2010.
